ABOUT US

The New Citizen Network Group (NCNG) was created to welcome newly naturalized U.S. citizens from across the nation to Philadelphia — the birthplace of America. Our goal is to build a tradition where new Americans visit the city to learn, explore, and celebrate the values of freedom, democracy, and unity.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to connect new citizens with the historical and cultural foundations of the United States. We aim to create meaningful experiences that strengthen civic pride and foster a sense of belonging within the American community.

OUR VISION

We envision Philadelphia becoming the number-one gathering place for new Americans — a city where every new citizen feels welcomed, inspired, and empowered to participate fully in civic life.

WHO WE SERVE

• Newly naturalized U.S. citizens
• Families traveling to Philadelphia
• Students and educators
• Community and cultural organizations
• Tourism partners

WHY PHILADELPHIA?

Philadelphia is where the Declaration of Independence and the U.S. Constitution were signed. For new citizens, it represents the heart of American freedom. By visiting the city, new Americans can walk the same streets where the nation began and connect their personal story with the broader American story.

OUR STORY

The New Citizen Network Group was founded in 2025 with a simple belief: that new citizens deserve a meaningful way to celebrate their journey in the city where the American dream first took shape. Today, NCNG is building partnerships with organizations, leaders, and volunteers dedicated to welcoming new Americans.
